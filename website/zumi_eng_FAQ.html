Product FAQ

Which devices can I use Zumi AI with?
Zumi AI’s unplugged card coding can be used without any additional devices or internet connection. Entry and Python coding are available on PCs running Windows or macOS (firmware version v1.2.3 or higher).
Where can I download the Entry program?
Please visit https://playentry.org/ws/new and download the latest version of the “Hardware Connection” program.
How do I charge Zumi AI? Is fast charging supported?
Turn the device switch to “OFF” and connect it using a micro USB cable. Fast charging is not supported, and we recommend using a charger with a maximum output of 5V.
Is the battery sold separately?
The battery is not sold separately.
What is the target age group for Zumi AI?
Zumi AI is an intermediate-level kit. Unplugged coding is suitable for elementary students, while block coding and Python coding can also be learned by middle school students.
Does Zumi AI require assembly?
Zumi AI comes as a fully assembled product and does not require any additional assembly.
Can I use the camera while driving the robot?
Yes, you can. Download the “Zumi AI” app from the Play Store to control and explore various camera-based functions.
What is the product warranty policy?
● Main board: 1-year free warranty from the date of purchase
● Motors, battery, cables: Excluded from warranty as consumables
● Not covered under warranty: Change of mind, expired warranty period, negligence or misuse, unauthorized disassembly/modification, malfunctions caused by connecting third-party products, etc.
● Service requests: Use the [Technical Support & Service Request] menu on the Robolink website.

Education & Usage FAQ

Are lesson plans available for classroom use?
Yes. On the official Zumi website (zumi.co.kr), select “E-Manual” from the menu to access various educational resources, including curricula, textbooks, and subject-specific examples.
Is there teacher training or a usage workshop available?
*Yes. For groups or institutions, training can be scheduled upon request. (Additional details can be arranged if needed.)
Which school subjects can Zumi AI be integrated with?
You can check the E-Manual for examples that can be applied in various subjects such as Practical Arts, Ethics, English, Science, Math, Korean, and Social Studies.
Are there competitions or challenge events?
Zumi AI is used in various educational institutions and competitions, and depending on the event regulations, participation in different coding contests is possible.
Is Zumi AI certified or approved by the Ministry of Education?
Zumi AI was developed with the participation of active education experts and features verified curricula used in various public education programs such as “Digital Saessak” and “Neulbom School.”
Up to what level can Python be learned with Zumi AI?
You can implement a variety of AI functions with Python, from simple driving control to line tracing and camera recognition.
How do the AI features work?
Zumi AI supports various AI functions such as voice recognition, line tracing, and image classification.
